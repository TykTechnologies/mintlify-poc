The following Tyk components, created and maintained by the Tyk Team, are proprietary and closed-source:

* [Tyk Dashboard](/5.5/tyk-dashboard)
* [Tyk Developer Portal](/5.5/tyk-developer-portal)
* [Tyk Multi Data Center Bridge](/5.5/tyk-multi-data-centre)
